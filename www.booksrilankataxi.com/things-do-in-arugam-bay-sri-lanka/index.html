Experience the Magic of Arugam Bay

Arugam Bay, located on Sri Lanka’s east coast, is internationally renowned as a top surfing destination. The consistent waves and laid-back vibe attract surfers from around the world, making it a hotspot for adventure and beach lovers alike.

Aside from surfing, Arugam Bay offers a range of activities for travelers. Nature lovers can visit Lahugala National Park, where elephants roam freely, or explore the serene Kudumbigala Monastery, hidden within the dense jungle. Elephant Rock provides stunning coastal views and is an excellent spot for swimming and hiking.
