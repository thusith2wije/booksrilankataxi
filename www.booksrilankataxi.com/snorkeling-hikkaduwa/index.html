Explore the Hidden Gems of Hikkaduwa

Hikkaduwa Beach is a popular coastal destination in Sri Lanka, known for its golden sandy shores and vibrant nightlife. The beach is ideal for water activities like swimming, surfing, and snorkeling, and its laid-back atmosphere makes it a great spot for relaxation. Hikkaduwa also offers a range of beachfront accommodations, restaurants, and cafes.

Hikkaduwa Beach

Located off the coast of Hikkaduwa Beach, the Coral Gardens are a popular spot for snorkeling and diving. The vibrant underwater ecosystem is home to a variety of marine life, including colorful fish and coral reefs. The calm and clear waters make it an excellent destination for underwater exploration, providing a serene and beautiful experience.

Hikkaduwa Coral Gardens

The Hikkaduwa Turtle Farm is a conservation project dedicated to protecting endangered sea turtles. Visitors can learn about the various species of turtles and the efforts to safeguard them. The farm plays a vital role in the conservation of turtle eggs, and guests can often see hatchlings being released into the ocean, making it a meaningful and educational experience.
