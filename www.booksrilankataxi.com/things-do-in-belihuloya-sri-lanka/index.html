Explore the Natural Wonders of Belihuloya

Belihuloya, located in the central highlands of Sri Lanka, is a hidden gem known for its lush greenery, breathtaking waterfalls, and adventure-filled landscapes. Surrounded by mountains, rivers, and tea estates, this picturesque destination is perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. One of the highlights of Belihuloya is its impressive waterfalls, including Pahanthudawa, Surathali Ella, and Bambara Kanda—the tallest waterfall in Sri Lanka. These natural wonders offer stunning scenery and refreshing pools, making them ideal for relaxation and photography.

For those looking for adventure, Belihuloya offers excellent hiking and trekking trails, such as Horton Plains National Park and Nonpareil Estate. Whether you want to explore misty mountains, experience the diverse wildlife, or simply take in the fresh mountain air, Belihuloya has something for everyone. With its tranquil rivers, scenic viewpoints, and easy access to nearby attractions, Belihuloya is a must-visit destination for anyone exploring Sri Lanka’s hill country.
