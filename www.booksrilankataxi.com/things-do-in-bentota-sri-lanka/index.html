Top Attractions in Bentota

Bentota Beach

Bentota Beach

Relax on one of Sri Lanka’s most beautiful beaches. A golden-sand beach perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and enjoying stunning sunsets.

Madu River Safari

Madu River Safari

Explore the mangrove ecosystem on a scenic boat ride. This boat tour takes you through lush mangroves, small islands, and a cinnamon plantation.

Brief Garden by Bevis Bawa

Brief Garden by Bevis Bawa

Visit a historic and artistic garden retreat. Designed by Bevis Bawa, this beautifully landscaped garden is filled with sculptures, exotic plants, and artistic features.

Kosgoda Sea Turtle Hatchery

Kosgoda Sea Turtle Hatchery

Learn about turtle conservation efforts and release baby turtles into the sea. A sanctuary dedicated to protecting endangered sea turtles and educating visitors about conservation.

Lunuganga Estate

Lunuganga Estate

Tour the former home of renowned architect Geoffrey Bawa. A beautifully designed estate with stunning gardens and architectural brilliance.

Galapatha Raja Maha Viharaya

Galapatha Raja Maha Viharaya

An ancient Buddhist temple with fascinating carvings and history. A spiritual site featuring a sacred relic, historic murals, and peaceful surroundings.

Water Sports in Bentota

Water Sports in Bentota

Try thrilling activities like jet skiing, banana boat rides, and windsurfing. Bentota is a hub for water sports, offering a range of exciting activities on the lagoon and beach.

Discover the Coastal Charm of Bentota

Bentota, located along Sri Lanka’s southwestern coast, is a paradise for beach lovers, nature enthusiasts, and adventure seekers. Known for its pristine beaches and vibrant marine life, Bentota offers a mix of relaxation and thrilling experiences. Whether you're basking under the sun at Bentota Beach, taking a scenic Madu River Safari, or exploring the artistic beauty of Brief Garden, there's something for every traveler.

For those interested in history and culture, the Lunuganga Estate and Galapatha Raja Maha Viharaya provide insights into Sri Lanka’s architectural and spiritual heritage. Meanwhile, the Kosgoda Sea Turtle Hatchery allows visitors to engage with local conservation efforts by learning about and even helping release baby turtles into the sea. From water sports to cultural excursions, Bentota is a must-visit destination in Sri Lanka. Book a budget-friendly taxi today and explore this coastal gem at your own pace!
