Most visited places in Nuwara Eliya

Gregory Lake

Gregory Lake

A picturesque lake offering boat rides and scenic views. A must-visit spot for relaxation, water sports, and enjoying the cool climate.

Horton Plains National Park

Horton Plains National Park

Home to the breathtaking World's End cliff and diverse wildlife. A scenic hiking destination with misty plains, waterfalls, and rare flora and fauna.

Seetha Amman Temple

Seetha Amman Temple

A sacred Hindu temple with ties to the Ramayana legend. Believed to be the spot where Sita was held captive, this temple attracts many pilgrims.

Pedro Tea Estate

Pedro Tea Estate

Experience the art of Ceylon tea production. Take a guided tour through lush tea plantations and learn about the tea-making process.

Lover’s Leap Waterfall

Lover’s Leap Waterfall

A stunning waterfall with a romantic legend behind it. A scenic hike leads to this beautiful cascade surrounded by lush greenery.

Victoria Park

Victoria Park

A beautifully maintained garden perfect for a peaceful stroll. An ideal spot for nature lovers, featuring vibrant flowers and birdwatching opportunities.

Ambewela Farm

Ambewela Farm

Known as Sri Lanka’s Little New Zealand. Visit this dairy farm to see cows, taste fresh milk, and enjoy the lush green pastures.

Experience the Charm of Nuwara Eliya

Nestled in the central highlands of Sri Lanka, Nuwara Eliya is often called “Little England” due to its colonial-era charm, cool climate, and lush tea plantations. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural exploration, this picturesque town offers a wide range of experiences.
Nature lovers can explore Gregory Lake, a serene spot for boating and picnics, or hike through the misty trails of Horton Plains National Park to witness the dramatic drop at World's End. Tea enthusiasts can visit Pedro Tea Estate to learn about Sri Lanka’s world-famous Ceylon tea, while history and mythology lovers can pay a visit to Seetha Amman Temple, a site linked to the legendary Ramayana tale.

For those looking to unwind, Victoria Park offers a peaceful escape with its beautifully manicured gardens, and a trip to Ambewela Farm provides a chance to experience dairy farming in the scenic hills. If panoramic views are your thing, Moon Plains presents an unmissable vantage point to take in the breathtaking landscapes. With its charming colonial architecture, refreshing climate, and stunning natural beauty, Nuwara Eliya is a destination like no other.
