Explore the Hidden Gems of Arugam Bay

Arugam Bay, on Sri Lanka’s east coast, is a surfer’s paradise known for its world-class waves, laid-back vibe, and stunning beaches. Whether you're a pro or a beginner, this tropical hotspot offers perfect surf conditions, especially between May and September. Beyond surfing, enjoy beachfront cafes, scenic sunsets, and nearby nature reserves for an unforgettable coastal escape.

Just a short drive from Arugam Bay, you can witness wild elephants in their natural habitat. Visit Lahugala National Park or Kumana National Park, where these gentle giants roam freely near lagoons and grasslands. A sunrise or sunset safari offers the best chance to see elephants up close while also spotting other wildlife like crocodiles and exotic birds.
