Discover the Beauty of Hikkaduwa

Hikkaduwa is one of Sri Lanka’s most famous beach towns, known for its stunning coastline, rich marine biodiversity, and relaxed atmosphere. The main attraction here is Hikkaduwa Beach, a long stretch of golden sand lined with palm trees, beach cafés, and water sports activities. For those interested in marine life, Hikkaduwa Coral Reef offers an unforgettable snorkeling and diving experience. The reef is home to colorful fish, sea turtles, and stunning coral formations just a short swim from the shore. Nearby, the Turtle Hatchery provides an opportunity to learn about turtle conservation and even release baby turtles into the ocean.

Adventure seekers can visit Narigama Beach, a surfing hotspot with great waves and a laid-back vibe. For a touch of culture, the Seenigama Temple, located on a small offshore island, is an interesting spiritual site to explore.
