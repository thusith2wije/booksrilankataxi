Discover the Gem Capital of Sri Lanka

Ratnapura, known as the "City of Gems," is a fascinating destination offering a mix of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and adventure. As the heart of Sri Lanka’s gemstone industry, Ratnapura is a must-visit for those interested in sapphires, rubies, and traditional mining techniques. Beyond gems, Ratnapura boasts stunning waterfalls such as Bopath Ella, Katugas Ella, and Kirindi Ella, where visitors can enjoy serene landscapes and refreshing waters. The region is also a gateway to the Sinharaja Forest Reserve, a UNESCO World Heritage site teeming with unique wildlife and dense tropical rainforests.

For those seeking adventure, climbing Sri Pada (Adam’s Peak) offers an unforgettable experience with spiritual significance and breathtaking sunrise views. Nearby, Udawalawe National Park provides one of the best safari experiences in Sri Lanka, where you can witness elephants, leopards, and other wildlife in their natural habitat. With its rich history, natural wonders, and cultural significance, Ratnapura is a hidden gem waiting to be explored.
